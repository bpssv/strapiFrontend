Why choose Bharti Public School?
Every student at BPS can find his/her niche and his/her passion, whether it is through the traditional college preparatory courses offered, the various athletic opportunities, visual and performing arts events, or co-curricular activities. Students have a chance to get involved in the community and develop skills that help them to develop as strong, independent young adults. From student leadership to robotics, there are so many ways for students to become active members of the community.
Keeping in view the students’ safety, one can only visit the campus after the school hours with the permission of the management. You can also take a virtual tour of our ​campus here​!
Kindergarten: Applications are accepted as per the dates scheduled by the Directorate Of Education of Delhi. These admissions are based on a point system. ● Primary, Middle and Secondary: Applications are invited through mail (admissions.sv@bps.edu.in) in the month of March, subject to availability of seats. These admissions are based on the Entrance Test. Students scoring the highest marks, are selected and finally screened through interviews. ● For Class XI: Admissions to class XI are invited after the declaration of Class X CBSE Board Result. Provisional admissions are done on the basis of the Pre Board Mark sheet in the month of May; but are reviewed after the CBSE Result Declaration. Students must meet with the criteria adopted by the school stream wise. ○ Science stream: above 80% aggregate and 80% in Mathematics and Science each. ○ Commerce Stream: above 80% aggregate and 80% in Mathematics. ○ Humanities: above 75% aggregate and 75% in Mathematics, if opting for Economics and IP.
