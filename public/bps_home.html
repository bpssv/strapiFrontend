Our Story

Our Founding Fathers

1980 was the year, when sharing a common passion for teaching, Dr. S.L. Batra & Sh. H.C.B. Bharti opened the doors of BPS to their first batch of students.

School Shed

The indefatigable efforts of the founders reaped fruits and within seven years, BPS moved to Swasthya Vihar – its present dwelling. It was a humble brick ‘n’ mortar structure that became home to faculty and students alike.

State Teachers Award to the Founder

For his unparalleled dedication and selfless devotion towards academia, the founder-director, Dr. S.L. Batra was bestowed with the prestigious State Teachers’ Award.

Inauguration of School Campus at Swasthya Vihar

From 1980 to 1998, the school saw a meteoric rise from a humble structure to the present day state-of-the-art green campus. With changing times, the campus became more modern and functional in approach.

BPS opens its second branch at Mayur Vihar

BPS continued to flourish in leaps and bounds; unveiled its another branch at Mayur Vihar in 2004. The imposing structure is a testimony of the hard work of the think tank.

Silver Jubilee Celebrations at the National Stadium

Sunita Godara, National Marathon Champion facilitated the annual sports function at the National Stadium with participation of all students at BPS.

BPS inaugurates its third branch, Prakriti at Noida

The Bharti Group of Schools takes a stride and inaugurates Prakriti, a K-12 school with learner-centred curriculum and interdisciplinary learning methods

National Award given to Dr. Savita Arora

Our Principal, Dr. Savita Arora was the recipient of the prestigious National Award for Teachers and was facilitated by President, Pranab Mukherjee.

BPS unveils its sports arena, BPSEA

The Bharti Pro Sports Excellence Arena (BPSEA) boasts of a dedicated athletic-track, indoor cricket pitch, basketball and volleyball courts

School operations go ONLINE!

As BPS celebrates its 40th year anniversary, its operations including academics and activities go digital!
